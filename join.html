Join the Group
We are interested in working with very promising potential PhD students and Postdoctoral researchers with a deep interest in CADD – either code development or application.

We are also happy to host, virtually or on-site, visiting research students currently studying a relevant undergraduate bachelor’s degree or master’s programme.

If you are interested in applying for funding to join the group please contact Darren with a brief CV, cover E-mail detailing research interests and how your interests map to those of the group. As these fellowships are highly competitive, prospective applicants should get in touch well in advance of the deadline.
